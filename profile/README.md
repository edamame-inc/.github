# edamame

**Official Kintone Partner · Philippines · 2024 Cybozu Global Partner of the Year**

We're a small firm in Pasig, Metro Manila that runs enterprise Kintone deployments for fifty+ clients across the Philippines — including ORIX METRO, Daikin, Quipper, AEON Fantasy, A2 Network, and GMA Network. We scale by running our own operations on AI, then ship the same discipline to every client engagement.

## What we do

- **Custom Kintone apps** — CRM, inventory, HR, project management, operations dashboards. Bilingual EN/JP by default. Go-live within a week.
- **KinPlug plugin suite** — our own Kintone plugin line covering PDF generation, email, enhanced lookup, workflow. [kinplug.com](https://kinplug.com)
- **AI integration** — Claude-powered lead scoring, email drafting, document parsing, meeting summaries inside Kintone workflows.
- **JavaScript customization** — deep technical build-out when standard Kintone features hit their limits.
